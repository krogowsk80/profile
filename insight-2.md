# Risk & Scale Mitigation in Data Migration

Moving 20 years of accumulated data is a liability-heavy task. It requires more than just data movement tools; it demands rigorous governance and validation frameworks.
I focus on ensuring data integrity for up to 22,000 stakeholders by implementing strict validation gates and governance policies that prevent data swamps and ensure trust in the new platform.
