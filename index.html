Professional Profile

Legacy-to-Cloud: I understand the “on-premise DNA” (SQL Server, Oracle, Hadoop) and how to successfully translate it into modern, cost-efficient cloud architectures.

Leadership and Teams Growth: I excel at coordinating high-seniority teams (4+ Seniors/leads), ensuring that the technical execution remains aligned with the C-suite’s strategic goals.
