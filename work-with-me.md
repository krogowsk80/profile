# Work With Me

### Strategically Driven. Architecturally Sound.
I partner with CTOs, VPs of Data, and Business Owners to transform high-risk data environments into scalable, high-integrity assets. Whether you are battling legacy technical debt, a failing high-stakes project, or a lack of architectural direction, I provide the roadmap and the engineering rigor to move you forward.

## How I Add Value
I don't just "write code." I build frameworks that ensure your data is trustworthy, your costs are optimized, and your team is empowered.

### 1. Strategic Discovery & Architectural Audit

**Best for:** Organizations facing high cloud costs, slow performance, or a lack of data trust. A 2-4 week deep dive into your current state. I perform "Data Archeology" to uncover hidden bottlenecks, audit your spend, and deliver a comprehensive Modernization Roadmap.

**Outcome:** A clear blueprint for migration (e.g., Synapse to Databricks) and a risk-mitigation strategy.

### 2. The "Rescue & Stabilize" Intervention

**Best for:** Projects that are failing in production or missing critical business requirements. I lead a cross-functional "strike team" to stabilize failing environments. I implement CI/CD, fix broken data models, and restore stakeholder confidence.

**Outcome:** A functional PROD environment and a transition from "emergency mode" to a professionalized release lifecycle.

### 3. Solution Architecture & Lead Advisory

**Best for:** Scaling companies building new platforms who need "Architectural Guardrails." I act as your fractional Lead Architect. I design the Medallion architecture, select the toolstack (Databricks, PBI, Orchestration), and mentor your internal developers to ensure the build follows best practices from Day 1.

**Outcome:** A scalable, documented system that your internal team can eventually own without external dependency.

## Why Choose a Specialist?
**20+ Years of "Full-Stack" Data Experience:** I’ve moved from the server room to the boardroom. I understand the T-SQL bugs and the ROI concerns of the CEO.

**The "Exit-First" Philosophy:** My goal is to build a system so robust and well-documented that you no longer need me. I specialize in capability transfer, not long-term dependency.

**Cloud De-risking:** I have a proven track record of navigating complex security and political landscapes to move risk-averse organizations (Public Sector/Finance) to the cloud safely.

## The Engagement Process
*   **Initial Consultation:** A 30-minute high-level alignment call.
*   **Discovery Phase:** A fixed-fee engagement to assess technical debt and define the scope.
*   **Execution:** I lead the architectural build or the project rescue.
*   **Handoff:** Comprehensive documentation and training for your internal staff.
