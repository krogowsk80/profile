# The Engineering-Led Architect

Architectural designs must be grounded in reality. Because I spent over a decade as a Senior Data Engineer, I understand the "plumbing" of data (Python, SQL, ETL/ELT) as deeply as the "high-level vision."
This engineering-first approach ensures that the blueprints I design are not just theoretically sound but practically executable and maintainable by the engineering teams.
